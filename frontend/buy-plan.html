Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Do I need a powerful PC?

No! Morpheus streams games from our powerful servers, so you can play on any device with a good internet connection.

What internet speed do I need?

For 1080p streaming, we recommend 15 Mbps. For 4K streaming (Ultimate plan), we recommend 50 Mbps or higher.

Can I play on multiple devices?

Yes, you can switch between devices seamlessly. Your game progress is saved in the cloud.
